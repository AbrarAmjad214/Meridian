SECURITY<\/h1>\n

[\/vc_column_text]

<\/span><\/div><\/div><\/div>[vc_column_text css=”.vc_custom_1596438040814{padding-top: 20px !important;}”]<\/p>\n

Al Fajer Facilities Management has a dedicated safety and loss prevention division. Our security division has over 1500 highly skilled, licensed and certified employees to carry out various jobs.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Our security teams are driven by process-based surveillance methodology, ensuring movements are well recorded and monitored. From 24-hour security services along with CCTV surveillance, Gym attendants, static man guarding to lifeguarding and close protection security, we have all that is required to furnish your safety and loss prevention requirements. We ensure each security personnel are physically fit and compliant with SIRA certification, as per the regional norms.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

With a quality control and audit procedures in place, our services are maintained at the optimum level that ensures you are always protected.<\/p>\n

Lifeguard services for community swimming pools and beaches. Retail security services include security for malls, large retail outlets, standalone stores by way of loss prevention solutions implementation.<\/p>\n
