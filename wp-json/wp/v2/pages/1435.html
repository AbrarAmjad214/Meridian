Al Fajer FM’s engineering and maintenance division has over 350 trained and highly skilled professionals and technicians providing residential and commercial maintenance services in Dubai and across the UAE. We work for residential and commercial contracts for planned preventative maintenance (PPM), reactive work, and emergency maintenance support.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Al Fajer FM provides flexible and cost-effective solutions. We are proactive in our approach when it comes to your maintenance needs. We can help with comprehensive Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing (MEP) work. Our methods are designed to optimize the performance of your mechanical and electrical utilities and in turn, extend the lifespan of your assets.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Pest control services for an effective treatment to keep your living space healthy. Our preventive treatments are tested for its efficiency as per UAE municipality norms.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Landscaping services for adding to the aesthetic beauty of your property. We provide complete packages that can include infrastructural work, hard and soft landscapes, turf pitches and much more.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Water tank cleaning services to residential, commercial and industrial amenities. With our post cleaning test like Chlorine residual levels, Legionella testing, microbiological testing, E coli testing, etc. by authorized laboratories, we make sure to deliver a hygienic water storage system for your use.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Wide range of General maintenance services to cater the clients from their day to day household fit outs and maintenance works like general painting, carpentry and masonry works as per project requirements.<\/p>\n
