 <\/p>\n

<\/span><\/div>
<\/span><\/div><\/div><\/div>[vc_column_text css=”.vc_custom_1530033894140{padding-top: 20px !important;}”]<\/p>\n

In 2016, Al Fajer Facilities Management extended the portfolio of our Engineering Division by creating a new discipline, namely Texturized Paints. This department has some of the most highly skilled and trained applicators (painters) in the region.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

We work for both commercial and residential contracts. Our teams undertake painting works for all types of establishments, from Five-Star & Four-Star luxury hotel suites, commercial buildings to private palaces, villas and residences. Texturized panting is taken as a turnkey task right from consultation through to the final delivery of the finished product; our teams recommend the most appropriate products by interfacing the client requirements to the site conditions like internal or external moisture conditions etc. to create the most stunning finishes.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

We are proud to be trained directly by San Marco, Italy and pleased to represent them in the UAE region.<\/p>\n
