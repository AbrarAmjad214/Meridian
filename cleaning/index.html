CLEANING

CLEANING

Meridian Prime Facilities Management Services LLC Facilities Managements cleaning division is the largest division of the company. We have over 300 individuals trained as per the renowned BICS method, working on various cleaning services and projects for commercial and residential establishments across Dubai and the other emirates of the UAE.

 

This includes commercial cleaning for business establishments like cineplexes, corporate offices and buildings, airports, shopping malls and hotels. Residential cleaning for establishments like residential towers, communities, executive housekeeping and villas.

 

What is different about Meridian Prime Facilities Management Services LLC FM’s cleaning services division?

We consult with clients to develop bespoke cleaning programs and recommend solutions best suited for them.

We are committed to reducing the use of chemicals and water consumption while maintaining desired results.

We research, identify and bring the latest technology, cleaning materials and solutions into the middle east region to our clients.

We set KPI driven benchmarks with clients for projects in cleaning and housekeeping, which enables them to measure their returns on their investment with us.

We are working with hospitals and schools to develop clean and green services that use minimal chemicals while offering unmatched cleaning and hygiene levels.
