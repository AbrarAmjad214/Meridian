Meridian Prime Facilities Management Services LLC FM

ABOUT US

Our aim as a facilities management company is to ensure the integration of people, technology and systems to provide a reliable support system and this principle is the pillar of our foundation. Meridian Prime Facilities Management Services LLC Facilities Management is a results-driven company committed to lead the regional facilities management (FM) industry.

 

We plan to achieve this feat by delivering an experience to our clients that goes beyond services. We are driven by technology and sustainability to offer the right solutions to clients. We are known for our professionalism and our reliable services across the UAE and for our excellence in all aspects of facilities management.

Vision

Meridian Prime Facilities Management Services LLC FM will be recognized for quality and customer excellence and be the benchmark for the FM industry in UAE.

MISSION

Be a trusted partner for our clients to foster an environment of innovation and service excellence. To maintain high ethical and professional standards, as we strive for sustainability and an exceptional learning and working environment.
